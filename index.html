About Spider-Man

Spider-Man is a boy bitten by a spider, who chooses to protext those who need him using the powers of a spider!! Spider-Man was created by the late and great Stan Lee.

  1. He is a super hero, seen as a menace by the infamous reporter J Jonah Jameson, editor-in-cheif of the Daily Bugle
  2. He has the ability to swing from buildings using webs, they are also pretty handy for dealing with enemies.
  3. Doing what he can for his Neighbour hood
  4. He can also climb up walls effortlessly!
